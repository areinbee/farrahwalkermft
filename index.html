About

I am an Associate Licensed Marriage & Family Therapist and a graduate of the Marriage and Family Therapy Masters Program at Northwestern University. I gained experience as a clinician with the Betty D. Harris Family Clinic located at the Family Institute and I currently work as a Clinical Therapist at Spring Tree Counseling. I work with adults and families with an emphasis on couples in conflict, multi-generational families and interracial and multicultural couples. In addition, I work with individual women who are navigating transitions such as retirement, grief, employment difficulties or divorce.

I believe change can best occur when therapy engages the entire family system. I enjoy working collaboratively with couples and individual clients to help identify and address presenting challenges. I seek to provide a safe place in my counseling office for clients to engage in change, develop new strategies and approaches to long standing matters. I value working with client feedback and integrating various theoretical approaches and techniques that uniquely fit client situations.
