After gaining invaluable experience working there, in the Summer of 2020, I started Enriched Living Counseling LLC as a solo practice.

I work with individuals and couples with an emphasis on infidelity, couples in high conflict, interfaith, interracial, and multicultural couples. In addition, I work with couples and individuals who are navigating transitions such as dating, marriage, retirement, grief, and divorce.

Prior to returning to graduate school in 2014, I spent my first career working in Criminal Justice, Juvenile Justice and Social Services. After ten years of working with this population, I intentionally decided to pursue Marriage and Family therapy to strengthen families and improve the quality of life for couples and individuals in mental and emotional distress.

I enjoy working collaboratively with couple and individual clients to help identify and address presenting challenges. I seek to provide a safe place for clients to engage in change and develop new strategies and approaches to long standing matters. I value working with client feedback and integrating various theoretical approaches and techniques that uniquely fit client situations. Finally, I use humor and life experiences to aid clients in creating their own narrative and a life rich with connection, inner peace, and joy.
